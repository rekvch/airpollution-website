INTRODUCTION

Filthy air here, stale air there, and stale air everywhere. Back then, the wind in Manila was ordinary, but today's air is tainted by a variety of dust, chemicals, and other pollutants. It arrived and was noticed by everyone in recent weeks since the entire Metro Manila was enveloped in smog, and many people assumed that it was caused by the eruption of Taal volcano, but it turns out that they were incorrect.

The smog was caused by thermal inversion, which causes air pollution. So, what exactly is air pollution? What are its causes and consequences? And what are the solutions and preventative measures for that specific problem? So, let us proceed into our advocacy, and turn our last breath into a fresh breath via FundTreesAir.
