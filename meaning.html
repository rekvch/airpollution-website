Air Pollution

What is Air Pollution?

Air pollution is a type of pollution that is made out of gas and chemicals caused by human activities such as transportation, industrial process and fossil fuels . It has harmful substances in the air which can negatively affect human health and the environment, by human health the polluted air can lead you to bad conditions with your respiratory and cardiovascular that will increase the chances of developing a disease. While environmentally, air pollution can harm ecosystems and cause a impact to climate change that will also affect humans.
